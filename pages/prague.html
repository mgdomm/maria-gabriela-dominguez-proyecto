Prague

Prague is the capital of the Czech Republic and the largest city in the Bohemian region. It has a privileged location in the center of Europe and is very well connected with neighboring countries, which makes it one of the most visited destinations on the continent. Prague is made up of the union of five ancient cities and is washed by the waters of the Vltava (Moldava) River. It has traditionally been the political, cultural and economic center of the Czech Republic.
